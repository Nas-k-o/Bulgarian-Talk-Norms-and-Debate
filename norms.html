Обобщителни упражнения върху книжовните норми

1. What is the primary goal of constructive feedback?





Explanation: The main purpose of constructive feedback is to provide specific, actionable suggestions that help the recipient understand areas for development and how they can improve their performance or behavior. While it might point out flaws, the intent is positive and growth-oriented.

2. During a debate, what does 'active listening' involve?





Explanation: Active listening means focusing completely on the speaker, understanding their message, comprehending the information, and responding thoughtfully. It's about absorbing what's being said, not just preparing your own points.
